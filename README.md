# Celebrity Wealth Analysis

A research repository covering how celebrities actually build wealth — beyond the headline net worth figure, into the income structures, ownership decisions, and financial patterns that determine real outcomes.

## Overview

Most celebrity net worth coverage focuses on the estimate. What's more interesting is the architecture underneath it — the income streams that built it, which are still active, and what the structural vulnerabilities are.

This repository documents research and analysis published at WealthArchives.com.

## The 6 Income Pillars of Celebrity Wealth

1. **Primary career income**
   The foundation — but rarely the whole story. Longevity and leverage determine how far it goes.

2. **Royalties and passive IP income**
   Where real wealth separation happens. Entertainers who own their intellectual property generate income long after the active work is done.

3. **Brand deals and endorsements**
   Audience trust matters more than follower count. Engaged niche audiences command higher rates than large disengaged ones.

4. **Business equity**
   Annual income gets spent. Equity compounds. The wealthiest celebrities own, not just earn.

5. **Real estate**
   The most consistent wealth-building vehicle across every category of celebrity.

6. **The lifestyle cost**
   The factor that silently destroys wealth more than any other. Two celebrities with identical earnings can end up with wildly different net worths.
